Flexibility, is it important?

Yes, the more flexible you are the more rotation you will have. Just look at Rory Mcilroys backswing, super flexible, look at where his left shoulder is pointing behind the ball!! Insane, I could never get my left shoulder there. Nor would I want to push it there either, matter of fact you do not need to either. Have a look a Jon Rahms backswing, super short for "his" felxibility and he hits it a mile long too. Then that means you could too.. Work with what you have, not what you want because there is no need to frustrate yourself thinking you need to be as flexible like Rory. Rory does it because it is normal for him, so do what is normal for you. 👉
