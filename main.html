Perfect Golf Swing:

The objective..

My goal is to show you my version of the golf swing and how confusing it can all be, especially when one is looking to improve on what they have. I am also in this position of wanting to better my golf swing, to get the upper hand with my mates. Not to out drive them, like to show off as such. But to show myself how much better I can be and in doing this, will change my game for the better. So let me show you how I think you can benifit from my version of the perfect golf swing. I wont be showing you how I play, what I will be showing is a set up and method to swing properly.👉
