Perfect Golf Swing:

The hips

Your hips play a very important part in your golf swing, if they are not working. Then neither is the rest of your swing, so here is how we invite the hips to play. At the top of the back swing, open your hips all the way. Start off really slow, just to see where your hands are as the club gets nearer to the ball. A great swing drill, is do it in slow motion. It is a great way to understand how it works and the more you practice this, the better your brain remembers. So when it is time to do it at normal speed. Your hips need to move out the way of your hands, right from the top of the back swing. So your hands have a clear path so you can hit the ball. Hips open at impact, just like Tiger Woods has done in this image.. 👉
