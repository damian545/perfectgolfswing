Perfect Golf Swing:

What is the thing they all have in common?

The hips are open before impact and at impact, in simple terms. The speed on how fast you do open them is determind by your pace of the downswing only. During chipping, your hips are actively turning while you are swinging the club back and through judging the pace for the shot type. Same goes for pitching shots.. Most golf shots require you to open the hips, of varying degrees, the only exception is when you are putting. 👉
