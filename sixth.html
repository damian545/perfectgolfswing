Perfect Golf Swing:

Posture:

This is another crutial part of the golf swing, being in a balanced and powerful posture is needed. A bad posture will lead to numerous problems, so it is important to be aware of slouching. Having a good posture, will give you the best angle of attack. As opposed to a bad posture, which will give you a steep angle of attack. Not ideal for a good golf swing! If you have a tall enough mirror handy, you can have a look at your posture to see if it is ok. Steep and flat at the top of the backswing, is possibly caused by disconnection. Using arms only to get the backswing, shoulders and torso together gets you your backswing. 👉
