Perfect Golf Swing:

How it all starts:

Look at the static image, you will see that fig 1 is incorrect. This is because the person is using arms only to take the club back, out in front and to the back of the person. This will cause a whole lot of problems, especially if this person believes this is how the back swing works!! Fig 2 is showing the correct way to execute the back swing, by using the shoulders to take the club back. The only thing a static image can not show you, is the tempo. The back swing tempo is super critical and if it is too fast, it will be like Fig 1. Trying to play golf like that, is like trying to swim with no arms or legs. You are going nowhere fast, except to the bottom of the ocean. Now look at Tiger Woods in the other image. Nice medium tempo on the back swing and only increases the pace at the ball, this will stop you losing your balance from the top of the back swing.👉
